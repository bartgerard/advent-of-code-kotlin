# System of Linear Equations

$$A x = b$$

where

$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
a_{11} & a_{12} & \dots & a_{1n} \\
a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & a_{2n} \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\
a_{m1} & a_{m2} & \dots & a_{mn}
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
x =
\begin{bmatrix}
x_1 \\ x_2 \\ \vdots \\ x_n
\end{bmatrix},
\quad
b =
\begin{bmatrix}
b_1 \\ b_2 \\ \vdots \\ b_m
\end{bmatrix}.
$$
